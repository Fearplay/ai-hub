AI Hub - non-functional button inventory (state 2026-05-11)
===========================================================

This file is an application-wide audit of visible UI controls that do
not currently have a real business action behind them.

It intentionally does not list controls that are already interactive
and functional (for example controls with `.clicked.connect(...)` in
their local context), or native Qt controls that do not belong to our
business logic, such as dialog close buttons and scrollbars.

Legend:
  [missing-handler]   - the control is rendered, but clicking it has no effect
  [placeholder]       - the whole section or tab is a "coming soon" screen
  [decorative-only]   - a hoverable or button-like visual element with no action
  [partially-wired]   - the UI updates, but the core business action is missing

Minimum user-facing rule:
  When a feature requires input, at least one input must be provided
  before the action can run. For example, AI Bug Report needs text,
  an attachment, or both.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
CONCLUSION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

From the user's perspective, the most visible missing handlers are:

  1. The "Apply template" / "Download sample" buttons in AI Legal
     -> Templates. Both are central tab actions.
  2. Quick action rows in the right-hand context panel for AI Legal
     and AI Marketing. They look clickable, but nothing happens.
  3. Action chips in all demo sections (AI Marketing / AI Finance /
     AI Study). These sections are explicitly marked as static demos
     in comments and should be replaced by full pipelines later.

Suggested implementation order, from highest impact:

  1. AI Legal: Templates tab - Apply / Download sample. This requires
     a pipeline for sample document generation and saving the selected
     template, color, and font into STATE.
  2. Shared `quick_action_row` and `history_row` - extend their
     signatures with an `on_click` callback so sections can wire them.
  3. AI Legal: Drafts tab - add the "Open in full" dialog plus the
     Refine / Save draft buttons.
  4. AI Legal: Analysis tab - wire "Export PDF" / "Forward to inbox"
     in chat mode.
  5. Rewrite ai_marketing / ai_finance / ai_study into full AI
     pipelines that follow `.cursor/rules/ai-section.mdc`.
